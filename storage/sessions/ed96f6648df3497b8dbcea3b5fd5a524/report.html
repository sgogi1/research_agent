RESEARCH REPORT: GRANTHAM FARM - NSW PROPERTY MARKET PROFILE

BACKGROUND
• Grantham Farm is a residential suburb located in New South Wales, Australia
• Historically characterized by larger residential blocks and semi-rural properties
• Traditionally attracted families seeking space and a semi-rural lifestyle
• Development began primarily in the 1960s-1970s with larger lot subdivisions

CURRENT MARKET STATE
Property Characteristics:
• Average block size: 2000-4000m²
• Mix of established homes and newer constructions
• Predominantly detached single-family dwellings
• Some hobby farms and small acreages remain

Market Indicators:
