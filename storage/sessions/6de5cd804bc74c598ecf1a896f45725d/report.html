RESEARCH REPORT: HORNSBY NSW PROPERTY MARKET ANALYSIS

BACKGROUND
• Hornsby is a major suburban center located 19km northwest of Sydney CBD
• Historical development as a railway junction town since 1886
• Traditional role as administrative center of Hornsby Shire
• Mix of residential property types: apartments, federation homes, modern houses
• Significant green spaces including Berowra Valley National Park

CURRENT STATE (2022-2023)
Median Property Prices:
• Houses: $1.65M - $1.8M
• Apartments: $750K - $850K
• Rental yields: 2.8-3.5%

Market Characteristics:
• High owner-occupier ratio (~65%)
• Strong transport links (rail junction, multiple bus routes)
• Education hub with multiple schools
• Major shopping precinct
• Growing healthcare sector around Hornsby Hospital

KEY MARKET DEBATES
Infrastructure Impact:
• NorthConnex influence on accessibility
• Westfield redevelopment proposals
• Hospital precinct expansion plans

Demographics:
• Increasing density vs heritage preservation
• Growing Asian-Australian population influence
• Shift toward apartment living among younger buyers

RISKS & LIMITATIONS
Market-Specific:
• Interest rate sensitivity
• Aging housing stock in some areas
• Infrastructure capacity constraints
• Bushfire risk in some sections

Broader Economic:
• Sydney market cooling effects
• Lending restrictions
• Construction costs inflation
• Foreign investment regulations

FUTURE DIRECTIONS
Short-term (1-2 years):
• Moderate price growth expected
• Continued apartment development
• Infrastructure upgrade impacts

Long-term (5+ years):
• Transit-oriented development focus
• Healthcare precinct expansion
• Possible rezoning of industrial areas
• Greater housing density near transport hubs
